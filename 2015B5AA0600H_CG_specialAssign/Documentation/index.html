Hermite Curve

Hermite curves are very easy to calculate but also very powerful. They are used to smoothly interpolate between key-points (like object movement in keyframe animation or camera control)

To calculate 2D hermite curve, we need following vectors

  • P1: the startpoint of the curve
  • T1: the tangent (e.g. direction and speed) to how the curve leaves the startpoint
  • P2: he endpoint of the curve
  • T2: the tangent (e.g. direction and speed) to how the curves meets the endpoint
